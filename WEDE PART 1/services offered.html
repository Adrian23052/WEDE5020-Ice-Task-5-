The services we provide are as follows:

  • We provide a messenger and delivery service to Attorneys, Advocates, and small businesses in the Durban and surrounding areas.
  • Service and delivery of documents and parcels to Attorneys, Advocates, and various companies/individuals.
  • Filing of court documents at the Magistrate court, High Court, Labour court, Maintenance court.
  • Index and pagination of court documents.
  • Attend to lodging of documents and queries at the Masters office, Family Advocates office, and various courts.
